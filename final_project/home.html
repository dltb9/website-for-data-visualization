Our Ideas


Yelp dataset contains comprehensive data for each restaurant, basic information, location, user evaluation, special needs, etc. We will do some overall visual analysis of American restaurants according to those information, meanwhile, based on our life experience, we have chosen some facts that customers may be concerned about, carry out some particularity visual exploration. Hopefully, our result would give some useful guidance to customers when they try to find a perfect dinning place.
